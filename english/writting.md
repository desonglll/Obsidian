- #英语写作

## we should的高级替换

1. ought to do sth.
   应该做某事

2. be supposed to do sth.
   应该做某事

3. It behoves us to do sth.
   我们应送做某事

4. It is highly desirable if we...
   这是非常可取的，如果我们…

## first/firstly的高级替换

In the first place.

首先，从一开始

First and foremost.

首要的；第一

To begin with.

首先，起初

The most important factor that we have to take into consideration is...

我们必须考處的最重要的因素是．
